McCuber framework evaluate security by considering the factors that impact them.
	1. Protecting information
		a. Confidentiality: data encryption, identify proofing, two factors
		b. Integrity: check integrity like checksum
		c. Avaiability: users can acces data when needed, backups and keep OS up to date
	2. Protecting ingo in esch possible states
		a. Processing: data used to perform operation(updating database)
		b. Storage: HDD/SDD, USB
		c. Transmission: data traveling
	3. Protect data
		a. Awereness, training and education
		b. Technology: software and hardware solution make to protect info(firewall)
		c. Policy: administrative controls, incident response and practice guidelines
